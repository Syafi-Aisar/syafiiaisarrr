My Experience

Here are some of the experiences I've had in my journey.

Construction Worker
After finishing my SPM in 2022, I joined Tukul Besi Contractor as a part-time worker, with Sundays off. I was assigned to the electrical and plumbing teams, where I received hands-on training, starting from the basics until I became skilled in the tasks assigned to me.
Waiter at Hotel
I worked as a part-time waiter at Bangi Golf Resort Hotel, assisting in both the banquet department and Fantasia Restaurant. I took on this role during my semester breaks, earning an hourly wage of RM10.
Internship
I completed my internship at the Hulu Langat District Education Office, where I worked in the office sector of the Learning Department. During my two-month training, I learned how to manage files and handle office administration. My internship was supervised by the Director of the Hulu Langat District Education Office, Mr. Zaiazman.
FoodPanda Rider
I am actively working as a FoodPanda rider this semester, taking on shifts during my free time or when I don’t have classes. Using my Yamaha NVX motorcycle, I’m able to earn extra pocket money, which helps me support myself and ease the financial burden on my family.
